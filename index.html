Who is eligible to vote in the Presidential Election?

In order to qualify as a voter, one must:

  • Be a Zimbabwean citizen

  • Be 18 years old and above

  • Be registered as voter

Who is eligible to vote in the House of Assembly Constituency Election?

In order to qualify as a voter, one must:

  • Be a Zimbabwean citizen

  • Be 18 years old and above

  • Be registered as voter in the constituency in question

Who is eligible to vote in the Local Authority Election?

In order to qualify as a voter, one must:

  • Be a Zimbabwean citizen

  • Be 18 years old and above

  • Be registered as voter

  • Resident in ward

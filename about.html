About

GoToVote! ZW is designed to help Zimbabweans find their nearest polling station, so that they know where in their respective ward to vote in the 2013 harmonised elections.

GoToVote! ZW is an example of how open data can be useful to ordinary citizens.

GoToVote! ZW demonstrates how data-driven tools can help media audiences act on the news they read ,watch, by finding out how a national event such as the elections affects their personal lives or local communities.

GoToVote! ZW is inspired by similar initiatives in Ghana,South Africa and Kenya.The original data for

GoToVote! ZW was extracted from Excel sheets provided by the Zimbabwe Electoral Commission (ZEC).
